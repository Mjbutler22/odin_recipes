Panko-Crusted Air Fryer Mahi Mahi

What is Mahi Mahi?

This delicious air fryer mahi mahi with panko breading has a satisfying crunch while the fish fillets turn out perfectly flaky. Serve with a side of rice or use the fillets for fish tacos.

Ingredients:

  • 4 (4 ounce) mahi mahi filets
  • 2 tablespoons grapeseed oil
  • 2 cups panko bread crumbs
  • 1 teaspoon everything bagel seasonsing
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • nonstick cooking spray
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 medium lemon, cut into 4 wedges

Directions:

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 5 minutes.

  2. Step 2

    3. Meanwhile, place mahi mahi fillets on a platter and drizzle with grapeseed oil.

  3. Step 3

    4. Mix panko, bagel seasoning, garlic salt, turmeric, and pepper together in a single layer in the air fryer basket. Spray with nonstick spray.

  4. Step 4

    5. Cook in the preheated air fryer until fish flakes easily with a fork, 12 to 15 minutes, flipping halfway through.

  5. Step 5

    6. Remove from the air fryer. Garnish with parsley and lemon wedges. Serve immediately.
